🤖 NEET Allotted List Prediction and Analysis using NLP

This project addresses the need for prospective medical students to understand their chances of being allotted a specific college based on their NEET rank, quota, and category.

The project utilizes Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques with the Deepset RoBERTa model for question-answering tasks to analyze the relationship between NEET ranks and college allotments.

The model is built upon the NEET allotted list, allowing it to predict institutes based on user inputs. The results demonstrate that the model can effectively predict the allotted college for a given rank, quota, and category. Users can also find the required rank for specific institutes or explore possible colleges based on their rank, improving the overall decision-making process for students.

📊 College Prediction and Visualization using Tableau

This project aims to provide a visual representation of the likelihood of being allotted to a college based on a student's NEET rank.

The project utilizes Tableau to categorize the chances of college allotment into three levels: "HIGH," "MEDIAN," and "LOW," based on the user's NEET rank. Colleges in all three categories are ordered from highest to lowest based on relevant criteria, including UG intake, total beds, PG intakes, total MBBS seats, and first-round closing rank.

The visualization effectively categorizes colleges based on the probability of allotment, providing users with a ranked list of institutions across all categories. This allows students to focus on the most favorable options available based on their rank and enhances their understanding of the admission landscape.
